Key Analyze Phase Conclusions 🔍

Emergency Department overcrowding is primarily driven by workflow and capacity-management issues
Minor cases significantly consume resources needed for urgent patients
Evening shifts represent the highest operational risk period
Admission delays directly impact Length of Stay and congestion
Real-time operational visibility is insufficient for proactive management
Fast Track implementation is operationally and statistically justified
