04 — Training & Handover
Health Holding Company | Control Phase

Purpose: To ensure Emergency Department leadership and frontline staff have the knowledge, operational skills, and documentation required to sustain the improved workflows without continued support from the DMAIC project team.

Handover Package 📦

This folder contains all materials required for successful operational transition and long-term sustainability.

1. Training Modules 🎓

| Module | Target Audience | Purpose |
|---------|-----------------|----------|
| Module 1: Fast Track Workflow | Physicians & Nurses | Sustain low-acuity patient-routing process |
| Module 2: Standardized Triage | Triage Nurses | Maintain triage consistency and prioritization |
| Module 3: Queue Dashboard Usage | ED Leadership & Charge Nurses | Monitor operational KPIs and congestion |
| Module 4: Smart Staffing Coordination | Nursing Supervisors | Maintain staffing alignment during peak demand |
| Module 5: Patient Routing Protocol | Frontline Staff | Sustain standardized patient movement workflow |
| Module 6: Bed Escalation Process | Bed Management & ED Leadership | Coordinate admission delays and escalation |
