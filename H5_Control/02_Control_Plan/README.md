02 — Control Plan

Health Holding Company | Control Phase

Purpose:

A living operational document that defines what Emergency Department KPIs must be monitored, how they are measured, who owns them, and what escalation actions must occur if performance falls outside acceptable limits.

The Control Matrix 📊

The Control Plan matrix is the core deliverable of the Control phase. It directly links to the baseline metrics identified in the Measure phase and the operational improvements implemented during the Improve phase.


| Metric | Spec / Target | Measurement Method | Frequency | Process Owner | Out-of-Control Response Plan |
|---------|----------------|--------------------|------------|----------------|------------------------------|
| Door-to-Doctor Time | ≤45 min | Queue Dashboard / EHR Timestamp | Hourly | ED Supervisor | Escalate staffing and activate Fast Track overflow support |
| Triage Time | ≤5 min | Triage Tracking System | Shift-based | Nursing Supervisor | Open additional triage coverage immediately |
| Length of Stay (LOS) | ≤3 hrs | EHR LOS Report | Daily | ED Leadership | Escalate admission delays to Bed Management |
| LWBS Rate | ≤2% | Patient Tracking Report | Weekly | Quality Team | Conduct workflow review and congestion analysis |
| Patient Satisfaction | ≥90% | Patient Experience Survey | Monthly | Patient Experience Team | Investigate complaints and review staffing/workflow |
| Fast Track Utilization | ≥85% compliance | Fast Track Audit | Daily | ED Supervisor | Review patient-routing compliance |
| Staffing Coverage Accuracy | ≥95% | Staffing Schedule Audit | Weekly | Nursing Supervisor | Adjust staffing model based on demand trends |
| Bed Escalation Response Time | ≤30 min | Admission Escalation Log | Daily | Bed Management Team | Escalate unresolved delays to hospital operations |


Escalation Thresholds 🚨

| KPI Trigger | Immediate Escalation Action |
|--------------|-----------------------------|
| Door-to-Doctor >45 min | Activate surge staffing plan |
| Triage backlog >10 patients | Deploy additional triage nurse |
| LOS >3 hrs for 2 consecutive days | Bed escalation review meeting |
| LWBS increase >2% | Immediate patient-flow RCA |
| Fast Track congestion | Redirect staffing/resources |
