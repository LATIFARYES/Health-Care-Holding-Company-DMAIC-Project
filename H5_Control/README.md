D5 — Control Phase
Health Holding Company | DMAIC Project

The Control phase answers one question:
“How do we sustain the Emergency Department improvements and prevent returning to old workflows?”

This phase ensures that the operational improvements implemented during the Improve phase remain stable, measurable, and continuously monitored over time.

| الحل المنفذ | آلية الضبط | مالك العملية |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| مسار التدفق السريع (Fast Track) | تدقيق يومي لمؤشرات الأداء | مشرف قسم الطوارئ |
| التوظيف الذكي | مراجعة أسبوعية لجدولة الموظفين | مشرف التمريض |
| لوحة بيانات الطابور | لوحة مراقبة فورية (Dashboard) | الممرض المسؤول / القيادة |

Sustaining the Improvements 📊

Based on the solutions implemented during the Improve phase, the following controls will be established:

| Implemented Solution | Control Mechanism | Process Owner | Response Plan (If Out of Control) |
|----------------------|------------------|----------------|-----------------------------------|
| Fast Track Workflow | Daily Fast Track KPI audit | ED Supervisor | Escalate congestion and adjust staffing |
| Smart Staffing | Weekly staffing review | Nursing Supervisor | Redistribute staffing during peak hours |
| Queue Dashboard | Real-time monitoring dashboard | Charge Nurse / ED Leadership | Immediate operational escalation |
| Standardized Triage | Weekly triage compliance audit | Quality Team | Retraining and workflow review |
| Patient Routing Protocol | Random workflow observation audit | ED Supervisor | Immediate coaching and correction |
| Bed Escalation Process | Daily admission-delay review | Bed Management Team | Escalate unresolved admission delays |
