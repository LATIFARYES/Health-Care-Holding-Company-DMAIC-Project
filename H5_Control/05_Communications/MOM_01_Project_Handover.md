Key Discussion Points 🏥


The Emergency Department DMAIC project successfully achieved its primary operational goals.


Door-to-Doctor Time improved from 95 minutes to within the target of ≤45 minutes.


Triage Time improved from 15 minutes to ≤5 minutes through standardized triage workflows and staffing adjustments.


Length of Stay (LOS) was significantly reduced through improved patient routing and bed escalation coordination.


The Fast Track pathway successfully reduced congestion caused by low-acuity patient volumes.


The real-time operational dashboard improved queue visibility and operational response during peak periods.


Emergency Department leadership reviewed the finalized Control Plan, SPC monitoring structure, escalation procedures, and updated SOPs.


The Quality Improvement Team confirmed readiness to continue KPI monitoring and sustainability audits.


Hospital leadership commended the implementation team for achieving improvements without disrupting patient-care operations.
