Successful Closure of Emergency Department DMAIC Project

Dear Executive Team,

I am proud to announce the successful closure of the Emergency Department DMAIC Improvement Project.

Over the past 6 months, the team implemented several operational improvements including the Fast Track pathway, standardized triage workflow, smart staffing adjustments, and real-time queue monitoring dashboards. These initiatives significantly improved patient flow efficiency and reduced operational delays across the Emergency Department.

As a result, Door-to-Doctor Time was reduced from 95 minutes to within the target of ≤45 minutes, triage time improved from 15 minutes to ≤5 minutes, and patient satisfaction increased substantially through improved waiting-time management and workflow coordination.

The new operational workflows, Control Plan, SPC monitoring system, and updated SOPs have now been formally handed over to the Emergency Department Leadership Team for long-term sustainability and monitoring.

Thank you to everyone who contributed to the success of this initiative and supported the improvement journey.
