01 — Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Health Holding Company | Control Phase

Purpose: To document the new Emergency Department operational workflows and ensure all staff follow the same standardized procedures across all shifts and future onboarding.

Updated SOPs 📋

The following SOPs have been created or updated based on the operational improvements implemented during the Improve phase:


| SOP File | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|
| SOP_01_Fast_Track_Workflow.md | Standard workflow for managing low-acuity patients through Fast Track |
| SOP_02_Standardized_Triage_Process.md | Unified triage prioritization and escalation procedure |
| SOP_03_Queue_Dashboard_Usage.md | Instructions for monitoring patient flow and operational KPIs |
| SOP_04_Smart_Staffing_Escalation.md | Staffing adjustment process during peak congestion |
| SOP_05_Patient_Routing_Protocol.md | Standard patient movement and routing workflow |
| SOP_06_Bed_Escalation_Process.md | Admission-delay escalation and inpatient coordination procedure |


SOP Objectives 🎯

Standardize Emergency Department workflows
Reduce variation between shifts
Sustain improvements in waiting times and patient flow
Support onboarding and training of new staff
Improve operational consistency and accountability
