**Problem Statement Details** 💬

**Problem Description** 📁

What is happening?

The Emergency Department is experiencing severe overcrowding and extended patient waiting times. Minor cases are consuming physician capacity intended for urgent and critical patients, leading to delays in medical assessment, treatment, and discharge.


Where is it happening?

The delays occur throughout the Emergency Department workflow, particularly during triage, registration, and physician assessment stages. The issue is most visible during peak hours and high patient-volume shifts.


How big is the problem?

Door-to-Doctor Time is currently more than double the target. Patient Length of Stay remains significantly above acceptable levels, and overcrowding has increased the number of patients leaving before being seen. Patient satisfaction scores continue to decline due to excessive waiting and congestion.


DATA 📊

| Metric | Current Performance | Target Performance |
|---------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Door-to-Doctor Time | 95 minutes | ≤ 45 minutes |
| Length of Stay (LOS) | 4.6 hours | ≤ 3 hours |
| Left Without Being Seen (LWBS) | 8% | ≤ 2% |
| Patient Satisfaction | 78% | ≥ 90% |
| Triage Time | 15 minutes | ≤ 5 minutes |
| Critical Case Waiting Time | 2–3 hours | ≤ 1 hour |


IMPACT ⚠️

Financial Impact 💰

Increased operational costs caused by overcrowding and inefficient patient flow
Higher staffing pressure and overtime requirements during peak hours
Reduced capacity to handle additional patients efficiently
Increased resource utilization caused by prolonged patient stays
Potential financial penalties linked to quality and patient satisfaction indicators


Operational Impact ⚙️

Overcrowding in waiting and treatment areas
Delays in physician assessment for urgent and critical patients
Minor cases occupying resources intended for higher-priority patients
Staff workload imbalance during peak periods
Increased patient congestion affecting overall Emergency Department flow
Delayed discharge and treatment processes impacting bed availability
Reduced visibility of queue status and waiting times without real-time monitoring systems
