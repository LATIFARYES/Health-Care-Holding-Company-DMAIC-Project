VOC to CTQ (Critical to Quality)

| VOC | NEED | CTQ | METRIC | TARGET |
|-----|------|-----|---------|---------|
| "Waiting time is too long" | Faster access to physicians | Door-to-Doctor Performance | Door-to-Doctor Time | ≤ 45 min |
| "Critical cases are delayed" | Priority treatment for urgent patients | Critical Case Response | Critical Case Waiting Time | ≤ 1 hour |
| "The Emergency Department is overcrowded" | Efficient patient flow | Patient Flow Efficiency | Length of Stay (LOS) | ≤ 3 hours |
| "Triage takes too long" | Faster patient prioritization | Triage Efficiency | Triage Time | ≤ 5 min |
| "We don’t know how long we will wait" | Queue transparency | Real-Time Waiting Visibility | Waiting dashboard availability | Live updates |
| "Minor cases delay urgent patients" | Separate treatment pathways | Fast Track Effectiveness | Fast Track utilization rate | ≥ 90% eligible cases |
| "Peak hours are difficult to manage" | Better staffing balance | Staffing Optimization | Peak-hour staffing coverage | ≥ 95% coverage |
| "Patients leave before treatment" | Reduced patient abandonment | Patient Retention | Left Without Being Seen (LWBS) | ≤ 2% |
| "Service quality differs between shifts" | Consistent operational performance | Process Standardization | KPI variation between shifts | ≤ 10% variation |
| "Patient satisfaction is low" | Better patient experience | Patient Satisfaction | Satisfaction Score | ≥ 90% |
