
Stakeholder Identification

Health Holding Company | Emergency Department DMAIC Project

| STAKEHOLDER | TYPE (INTERNAL/EXTERNAL) | ROLE | INFLUENCE AREA |
|--------------|---------------------------|------|-----------------|
| Hospital Management | Internal | Oversees hospital operations and strategic priorities | Resource allocation, KPI approval, project sponsorship |
| Emergency Department Manager | Internal | Manages Emergency Department operations | Patient flow, staffing, operational execution |
| Emergency Physicians | Internal | Assess and treat emergency patients | Door-to-Doctor performance, treatment efficiency |
| Triage Nurses | Internal | Perform patient prioritization and triage assessment | Triage speed, patient prioritization accuracy |
| Nursing Team | Internal | Deliver patient care and support treatment workflow | Treatment flow, patient monitoring, discharge coordination |
| Quality Improvement Team | Internal | Monitors KPIs and process performance | Performance tracking, process compliance |
| Bed Management Team | Internal | Coordinates inpatient bed allocation | Admission delays, patient transfer flow |
| IT Department | Internal | Supports operational systems and dashboards | Real-time monitoring systems, reporting tools |
| Patients & Families | External | Receive Emergency Department services | Patient satisfaction, waiting-time feedback |
| Inpatient Departments | Internal | Receive admitted patients from ED | Bed availability and patient transfer efficiency |
