Risk Analysis ⚠️

| STAKEHOLDER | RISK TO PROJECT | RISK LEVEL | REASON | MITIGATION STRATEGY |
|--------------|-----------------|-------------|---------|----------------------|
| Emergency Physicians | Resistance to Fast Track workflow | High | Concern about increased workload and workflow disruption | Involve physicians in workflow design and pilot testing |
| Triage Nurses | Inconsistent triage classification | High | Variation in experience and triage decisions | Standardize triage protocols and provide training |
| Nursing Team | Staffing shortages during peak hours | Medium | High patient volume and workload imbalance | Implement smart staffing schedules |
| IT Department | Delays in dashboard implementation | Medium | System integration and technical limitations | Involve IT early and define clear system requirements |
| Hospital Management | Resource reallocation to other priorities | Low | Competing operational priorities | Link KPIs to organizational performance goals |
| Bed Management Team | Delayed inpatient transfers | High | Limited bed availability | Improve admission coordination and escalation process |
| Patients & Families | Dissatisfaction during transition period | Medium | Operational changes may temporarily increase confusion | Improve communication and waiting-time visibility |
