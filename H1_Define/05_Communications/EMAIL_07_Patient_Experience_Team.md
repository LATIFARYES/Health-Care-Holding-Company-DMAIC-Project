As part of the Emergency Department DMAIC Improvement Project, we would like to request your collaboration and support in understanding patient concerns, expectations, and satisfaction drivers related to Emergency Department services.

* Extended waiting times before physician assessment
* Emergency Department overcrowding
* Delays in treatment and admission processes
* Reduced patient satisfaction scores

The project team would appreciate your support with:

* Access to patient satisfaction reports and complaint trends
* VOC (Voice of Customer) feedback related to Emergency Department services
* Identification of common patient concerns and recurring themes
* Support in designing patient-focused improvement opportunities
* Participation in workflow review and feedback sessions

Key patient-experience metrics requested include:

* Patient Satisfaction Scores
* Complaint Categories and Trends
* Waiting-Time Feedback
* Communication and Service Experience Feedback

All collected information will be used solely for operational improvement and service-quality enhancement purposes.

We highly value your partnership and contribution to improving patient-centered care and Emergency Department performance.
