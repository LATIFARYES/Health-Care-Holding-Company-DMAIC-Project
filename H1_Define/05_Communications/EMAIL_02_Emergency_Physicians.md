Dear Emergency Department Physicians,

As part of our ongoing efforts to improve Emergency Department operations and patient experience, we are launching a DMAIC improvement project focused on reducing waiting times, improving patient flow, and enhancing care efficiency.

Current operational challenges include:

* Door-to-Doctor Time averaging 95 minutes (target ≤45 minutes)
* Emergency Department overcrowding during peak hours
* Delays in treatment for urgent and critical patients
* Increased patient dissatisfaction related to waiting times

Your role is critical to the success of this project.

The project team will work closely with physicians to:

* Understand workflow challenges during daily operations
* Identify bottlenecks affecting patient assessment and treatment
* Evaluate opportunities for Fast Track implementation
* Improve coordination between triage, treatment, and discharge processes

What we need from you:

* Participation in workflow discussions and feedback sessions
* Support during process observation and pilot testing
* Input regarding operational challenges and improvement opportunities
* Collaboration in developing practical and sustainable solutions

Please note that this initiative is focused on process improvement and operational efficiency — not individual performance evaluation.

A project kickoff session will be scheduled next week to review objectives, timelines, and expected outcomes.

We highly value your expertise and contribution to improving patient care and Emergency Department performance.
