# Key Findings Discussed

- Peak-hour congestion significantly increases waiting times
- Minor cases are occupying treatment areas intended for urgent patients
- Delays in inpatient admissions are extending Length of Stay
- Staffing levels are inconsistent during high-demand periods
- Triage workflow variation exists between shifts
- Lack of real-time queue visibility affects operational decisions
