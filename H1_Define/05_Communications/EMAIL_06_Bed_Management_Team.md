
To: Bed Management Team
From: Project Lead — DMAIC Improvement Team
Date: July 4, 2026

Dear Bed Management Team,

As part of the Emergency Department DMAIC Improvement Project, we would like to request your collaboration in improving patient transfer coordination and inpatient bed allocation processes.

Current operational data shows that delays in inpatient admissions and bed availability are contributing significantly to Emergency Department overcrowding and extended patient Length of Stay (LOS).

Current performance gaps include:

* Length of Stay (LOS) averaging 4.6 hours (target ≤3 hours)
* Delays in patient transfers from the Emergency Department
* Increased congestion during peak admission periods
* Reduced treatment capacity caused by occupied Emergency Department beds

Your team plays a critical role in supporting patient flow and reducing Emergency Department bottlenecks.

The project team will work closely with Bed Management to:

* Review current admission and transfer workflows
* Identify delays affecting inpatient bed allocation
* Improve communication between departments
* Support faster patient movement from the Emergency Department to inpatient units
* Enhance real-time visibility of bed availability

What we need from you:

* Participation in workflow review sessions
* Access to bed allocation and transfer data
* Support in identifying operational barriers affecting patient movement
* Collaboration during pilot testing and process improvements

Please note that this initiative focuses on improving operational efficiency and patient flow across the hospital system.

We appreciate your support and partnership in improving Emergency Department capacity and patient care.

Best regards,

Project Lead
DMAIC Improvement Team
