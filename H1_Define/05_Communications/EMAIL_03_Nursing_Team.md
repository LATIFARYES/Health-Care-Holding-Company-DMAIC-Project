
To: Emergency Department Nursing Team
From: Project Lead — DMAIC Improvement Team
Date: July 2, 2026

Dear Nursing Team,

We are pleased to inform you about the launch of the Emergency Department DMAIC Improvement Project focused on reducing waiting times, improving patient flow, and enhancing overall Emergency Department efficiency.

The Emergency Department is currently facing several operational challenges, including:

* Long patient waiting times
* Overcrowding during peak hours
* Delays in triage and treatment processes
* Increased workload pressure on frontline staff

Because nurses play a central role in patient triage, coordination, and treatment flow, your participation is essential to the success of this initiative.

The project team will work closely with the nursing staff to:

* Understand current workflow challenges
* Identify delays and bottlenecks during triage and patient movement
* Improve communication and coordination across shifts
* Support implementation of Fast Track pathways for minor cases
* Explore smarter staffing and workload balancing strategies

What we need from you:

* Participation in workflow observations and feedback sessions
* Support during pilot testing and improvement implementation
* Suggestions for improving patient flow and reducing delays
* Collaboration in maintaining standardized triage and patient-handling procedures

Please note that this project is intended to improve systems and operational processes, not evaluate individual performance.

A kickoff session will be arranged next week to discuss project objectives, timelines, and upcoming activities.

We appreciate your continued dedication to patient care and your support in improving Emergency Department operations.

Best regards,

Project Lead
DMAIC Improvement Team
