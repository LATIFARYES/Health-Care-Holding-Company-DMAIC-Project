Data Extraction Request (IT Department)

Dear IT Team,

As part of the Emergency Department DMAIC Improvement Project, we are currently establishing baseline operational measurements for the Measure phase.

We kindly request extraction of Emergency Department operational data covering the last 6 months (January 01 – June 30, 2026).

Required Data Fields:

* Patient Arrival Date and Time
* Triage Category and Triage Time
* Door-to-Doctor Time
* Length of Stay (LOS)
* Left Without Being Seen (LWBS)
* Admission Delays and Bed Assignment Times
* Staffing Coverage by Shift
* Patient Queue and Waiting-Time Data

Preferred Format:
Excel (.xlsx) or CSV

Deadline:
July 15, 2026

This data is critical for validating current operational performance and identifying process bottlenecks affecting patient flow and waiting times.

Thank you for your cooperation and support.

Best regards,
Project Lead
DMAIC Improvement Team



Manual Data Collection Instructions (Emergency Department Supervisors)

Dear Emergency Department Supervisors,

Starting next week, the DMAIC Improvement Team will begin workflow observations and manual time studies within the Emergency Department as part of the Measure phase.

The purpose of these observations is to better understand:

* Triage delays
* Patient movement bottlenecks
* Waiting times during peak hours
* Workflow variation between shifts

The project team will observe operational processes including:

* Patient arrival and registration
* Triage assessment
* Door-to-Doctor process
* Bed assignment and admission flow
* Fast Track routing opportunities

Please note:
This activity is NOT an evaluation of individual staff performance. The focus is on measuring and improving operational processes and patient flow.

We kindly request your support in coordinating with frontline teams and maintaining normal operational procedures during the observation period.

Thank you for your cooperation and continued support.

Best regards,
Project Lead
DMAIC Improvement Team
