Key Measure Phase Findings 🔍

Door-to-Doctor Time remains consistently above the ≤45-minute target
Minor and non-urgent cases significantly contribute to overcrowding
Peak-hour staffing does not match patient arrival patterns
Admission delays increase Emergency Department Length of Stay
Triage performance varies between shifts
Patients leaving without being seen increase during evening congestion
Lack of real-time queue visibility slows operational response
Fast Track opportunity identified for low-acuity patients
