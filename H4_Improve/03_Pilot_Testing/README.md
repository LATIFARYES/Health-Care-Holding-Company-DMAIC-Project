03 — Pilot Testing
Health Holding Company | Improve Phase
Purpose: To test high-impact Emergency Department improvement solutions on a small scale, evaluate operational effectiveness, and refine workflows before full implementation across the department.

Pilot Scope & Criteria 🧪
Not all solutions require pilot testing.
Pilot testing is used for solutions that:


Significantly change operational workflow


Affect physician or nursing behavior


May disrupt patient flow if unsuccessful


Require operational coordination across multiple teams



Pilot Plan: Fast Track Pathway 🚨
| Item | Details |
|------|----------|
| Pilot Area | Emergency Department – Low-Acuity Cases |
| Duration | 2 Weeks |
| Scope | ESI Level 4 & 5 patients |
| Staffing | Dedicated physician + nurse |
| Success Criteria | Door-to-Doctor Time ≤45 min |
| Data Collection | Waiting time, LOS, queue length, LWBS |

Pilot Objective
Separate low-acuity patients into a dedicated workflow to reduce congestion affecting urgent and critical patients.
