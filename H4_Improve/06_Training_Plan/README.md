Training Plan — Improve Phase
Health Holding Company | DMAIC Project


This folder contains the comprehensive training plan designed to ensure successful adoption of the operational improvements implemented during the Improve phase. The training focuses on improving staff capability, workflow consistency, and operational confidence across the Emergency Department.

Training Modules Overview 📋

| Module | Target Audience | Objective | Duration |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|----------|
| TM-01: Fast Track Workflow | Physicians & Nurses | Apply the Fast Track process for low-acuity patients | 4 Hours |
| TM-02: Standardized Triage | Triage Nurses | Standardize triage and patient prioritization | 3 Hours |
| TM-03: Queue Dashboard Usage | ED Leadership & Charge Nurses | Monitor patient flow and waiting times using the live dashboard | 2 Hours |
| TM-04: Smart Staffing Workflow | Nursing Supervisors | Apply staffing adjustments based on patient-volume trends | 2 Hours |
| TM-05: Patient Routing Protocol | Frontline Staff | Improve patient movement and reduce bottlenecks | 3 Hours |
| TM-06: Change Management & Communication | All ED Staff | Improve adaptation to workflow changes and operational coordination | 2 Hours |

Training Methodology 🎓

The training program uses the “Tell – Show – Do – Review” methodology:

| Stage | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Tell | Explain the purpose of the workflow change and expected outcomes |
| Show | Demonstrate the process in the Emergency Department environment |
| Do | Staff perform the workflow under supervision |
| Review | Immediate coaching, feedback, and competency validation |

Training Objectives 🎯
Reduce workflow variation between shifts
Improve triage consistency and patient prioritization
Increase staff confidence in new workflows
Improve operational coordination during peak hours
Support sustainable reduction in waiting times and overcrowding
